How to withdraw DBT

1

Install Metamask

You can download Metamask in this link: https://metamask.io/download.
After download, install and configure your account.
IMPORTANT: Make a backup of your seed
2

Connect to Harmony (ONE) Network

Access https://viper.exchange/#/swap.
On top menu, click on "Connect a Wallet".
Give all permissions.
Ready
3

Import DBT Token to Metamask

Go to the Metamask extension
Click "Add Token"
Enter the token contract address in the "Token Contract Adress" field

DBT Contract Address: 0xD322822b4e8b9F0b3120AC93D80FD567422d13d9

Confirm that the token symbol is DBT and that decimals of Precision is 18
Click "Next"
Click "Add Tokens"
4

Withdraw

Logged on game, click "withdraw" button.
Paste your metamask address
Select amount
Confirm
Ready :D
